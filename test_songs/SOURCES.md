# Test song sources and public-domain basis

All four are acoustic-era 78 rpm recordings digitized by the Internet
Archive's George Blood / 78rpm collection. Each file here is trimmed to
the first ≤90 seconds and downmixed to mono (96 kbps MP3) with ffmpeg.

**Public-domain basis (US):** under the Music Modernization Act, all
sound recordings first published before January 1, 1923 entered the
public domain on January 1, 2022. Every recording below was published
1905–1913. The underlying compositions (1899–1910) are public domain as
pre-1923 publications. Both layers — composition and recording — are
therefore public domain.

Dates are from each Internet Archive item's `date` metadata field
(taken from the original record labels/discographies). The recordings
are noisy and mid-heavy, as expected from acoustic-horn 78s — that is
part of why they make a realistic stress test for the analysis pipeline.
